I received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Technology from [Shenzhen Technology University](https://www.sztu.edu.cn/) in June 2024. Currently, I am a research assistant under the supervision of [Prof. Xiaomao Fan](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=zDwIj2wAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN). My research interests include medical image analysis, multimodal learning, and computer-aided diagnosis.

You can view my CV here: [VVJia's Curriculum Vitae](../assets/Curriculum_Vitae.pdf).
